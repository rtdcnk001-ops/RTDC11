Our Mission

This institution is at your service with a programme of practical technical training in order to contribute to the development of the country and society at large. It is registered under the clause 21 of UP government societies act 1860 and copyright Government of India and QMS ISO 9001-2015 also. Who makes himself an employer.

Today at the international level our country is known as a developing country, while many other countries which attained freedom much later than we and which are almost equal in size with a province of our country, are designated as Developed countries. Today we take pride in importing and selling the items produced on the basis of their technology, while it is a matter or that every developed country has its educational policy based more and more on technical education. In our country, on the other hand, we have a tradition of acquiring degrees and running diligence, skill and self-confidence. We run after the government services only for the reason that if once settled in service we shall be earning the salary throughout the life even if we choose to do little work, We are prepared to spend any amount of money for the government service. Services run after them. Such people usually offer jobs to others. In developed countries the students, as soon as they complete their primary education, are classified on the basis of their learning and aptitude and promoted in the areas of technical or academic education. Unfortunately, we take no notice of this and therefore we stand in the category of developing nations. This institute sincerely appeals to the guardians to have an evaluation of the talent of their wares and to encourage them in the right field of education at the right time, so that our country may progress in the true sense of the world.

Our Vision

This institution is conduction programme of technical training with efforts to provide the students at the place nearest to them, with the same standard of education for which they rush to the cities like Jaipur, Mumbai and Delhi and waste their valuable time and money. There are, of course, a number of institutions engaged in imparting technical education, but they fail to satisfy the students, who have ultimately to go to the aforesaid cities, This institution, on the other hand, has already undertaken and it will continue to do so and evaluation of technical specialities of different institution through a Survey team sent to various cities of India. It has set its course of action on the basis of advanced techniques and systems of the present days. Our experts are receiving at present, and will continue to receive in future, the highest technical training available all over India on the expenses of the institution, in order to safeguard the students against every possible inconvenience.

The institution particularly solicits the guardians to pay a visit of it and to verify themselves whether we have executed our Claims in action or not and in case they feel that we have not fulfilled any one of them they may kindly oblige us by their valuable suggestions and cooperation.
